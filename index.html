Welcome to Tattoo House Nepal, where art meets skin. For over 20 years, our talented artists have been transforming ideas into stunning tattoos, capturing significant moments for our clients. We offer a clean, welcoming environment where creativity flourishes and each client feels valued. Our services include custom tattoos, cover-ups, and consultations, ensuring unique designs that reflect individuality. Our team specializes in a wide range of styles, from traditional to realism. With a strong focus on hygiene and exceptional customer service, we have built lasting relationships with our clients. Located in the heart of Kathmandu, Tattoo House Nepal is open seven days a week. In the quiet moments before the buzzing of the tattoo machine fills the room, I find solace in the anticipation of transforming skin into art. For over a decade, tattooing has been my life’s calling—a journey marked by creativity, craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to my clients' stories. Each tattoo I create is a testament to the power of expression and individuality. Whether it's a delicate rose etched on a wrist, a vibrant sleeve celebrating cultural heritage, or a minimalist design conveying profound personal meaning, every stroke is deliberate. My art is a collaborative effort, weaving together my client's aspirations with my artistic interpretation, creating a visual narrative that speaks volumes without words. Craftsmanship is at the heart of my practice. From meticulously sketching designs to the precise execution of each line and shade of ink, I strive for perfection. Each tattoo is not merely a piece of art but a lifelong commitment to quality and longevity. I take pride in mastering diverse styles—from intricate geometric patterns to the flowing lines of traditional Japanese art—pushing the boundaries of my creativity with every project.
Building trust with my clients is paramount. Every tattoo session begins with a conversation—an opportunity to understand their desires, fears, and aspirations. I aim to create a safe and welcoming space where they can express themselves freely, knowing that their vision is in capable hands. The joy and satisfaction I witness when they see their tattoo for the first time is my greatest reward—an affirmation of the trust they place in my artistry.
-Niraj Shrestha, Chief Artist
