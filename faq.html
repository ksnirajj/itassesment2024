FAQs about Tattoo House Nepal

What styles of tattoos do you specialize in?
At Tattoo House Nepal, we specialize in a wide range of tattoo styles, including traditional, realism, Japanese, geometric, and more. Our skilled artists can also work with custom designs to bring your unique vision to life.

Is it safe to get a tattoo at Tattoo House Nepal?
Yes, safety is our top priority. We adhere to strict hygiene practices, including using sterilized equipment, single-use needles, and maintaining a clean environment to ensure a safe tattooing experience.

How do I book a tattoo appointment?
To book an appointment, you can visit our studio in Kathmandu, call us at +977-9865593101, or click here. We recommend scheduling a consultation to discuss your design ideas and preferences.

Can you cover up or fix existing tattoos?
Absolutely! Our artists are experienced in cover-up tattoos and can help transform or enhance existing tattoos to better suit your current preferences or cover unwanted tattoos effectively.

What should I do to prepare for my tattoo appointment?
Before your appointment, make sure to eat a good meal, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol or blood-thinning medications. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area where you want to get tattooed.

How much does a tattoo cost?
Tattoo prices vary depending on size, complexity, and the artist's hourly rate. During your consultation, we'll provide a personalized quote based on your design and specifications.

Do you offer aftercare instructions for tattoos?
Yes, we provide detailed aftercare instructions to ensure your tattoo heals properly. This includes guidance on cleaning, moisturizing, and protecting your tattoo to maintain its vibrancy and minimize risks of infection.

Can I see examples of your previous work?
Absolutely! You can view our portfolio of past tattoo projects either on our website gallery or in person at our studio. We're proud to showcase our artists' skills and diverse range of tattoo styles.

Do you accept walk-in appointments?
While appointments are recommended to ensure availability and proper consultation, we do accept walk-ins based on artist availability. Contact us or visit our studio to check for same-day appointment options.

What if I have more questions or concerns?
If you have any additional questions or concerns about getting a tattoo at Tattoo House Nepal, feel free to reach out to us via phone, email, or visit our studio. Our friendly team is here to assist you every step of the way.
