About Us

Welcome to Tattoo House Nepal, your premier destination for high-quality, artistic tattoos in the heart of Kathmandu. For over 20 years, we have been at the forefront of the tattoo industry, providing our clients with exceptional designs and unparalleled service. Our studio is a vibrant space where creativity thrives, and every client is treated like family.
At Tattoo House Nepal, our team of skilled artists specializes in a diverse range of tattoo styles, from traditional and realism to Japanese and geometric. Whether you have a specific design in mind or need help conceptualizing your idea, we are here to bring your vision to life. Each tattoo is a collaborative process, ensuring that the final piece is not only visually stunning but also deeply meaningful to you.
Safety and hygiene are our top priorities. We adhere to the highest standards of cleanliness, using sterilized equipment and single-use needles to guarantee a safe tattooing experience. Our commitment to quality extends beyond the studio, as we provide detailed aftercare instructions to help you maintain the vibrancy and health of your tattoo.
We believe that every tattoo tells a story, and we are honored to be part of your journey. Our artists are not just experts in their craft; they are passionate storytellers who understand the importance of each line, shade, and detail. From intricate, personalized designs to bold, statement pieces, our goal is to create tattoos that you will cherish for a lifetime.
Located in the bustling area of New Baneshwor, our studio is easily accessible and welcomes clients from all walks of life. We are open seven days a week to accommodate your schedule and ensure you have the best possible experience. Our friendly and professional team is always here to answer your questions, provide consultations, and guide you through the tattooing process.
Thank you for choosing Tattoo House Nepal. We look forward to working with you and helping you express your unique story through beautiful, lasting art.
